# Data Retention Policy

1. **Purpose**: This policy outlines the information that is collected, stored, and retained by our cloud-hosted service when using the Sidekick plugin.

2. **Information Collected**: The following information is collected when the plugin is used: API key, request size, response size, IP address, and the date and time of the request. Information derived from the binaries loaded by Binary Ninja is governed by the [terms of service](terms-of-service.md).

3. **Retention of Information**: The information collected for providing the service will be retained for a period of 30 days from the date of the request. After this period, all information will be deleted from our servers, unless it is needed for billing purposes.

4. **Use of Information**: The information collected for providing the service will not be used or shared for any other purpose, nor will we sell it to any third party.

5. **User Consent**: By using the Binary Ninja Sidekick plugin, users consent to the collection, retention, and use of the information outlined in this policy.

6. **Changes to Policy**: This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website and will take effect immediately. We will make every effort to communicate any changes to our users in a transparent manner.

7. **Contact Information**: If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at [sidekick@vector35.com](mailto:sidekick@vector35.com). We value our users' privacy and will do everything we can to address any concerns they may have.
